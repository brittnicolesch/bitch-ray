# 9/11 Anomalies: DEA & Israeli Art Students

## The DEA Report (2001)
In January 2001, the DEA Office of Security Programs began receiving reports of "Israeli art students" attempting to penetrate DEA facilities.

## Connections to 9/11 and Latin America
- **Classic International Movers:** FBI documents reveal that four Israeli employees of this moving company "entered the U.S. from various locations in South America" and were detained after 9/11.
- **Telecom/Surveillance Intercepts:** One Israeli detainee's notebook contained a phone number "coincidentally subscribed to by an individual in South America with authentic ties to Islamic militants in the middle east."
- **Co-location:** The FBI investigated surveillance activities by Israelis spotted in North Carolina and suspected of keeping an apartment in California to spy on a group of Arabs. Reports indicated these "art student" cells lived in the exact same neighborhoods as Arab terrorist cells in Texas and Florida (e.g., Delray condos).
