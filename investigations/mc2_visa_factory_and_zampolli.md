# MC2 Visa Factory, Zampolli, & The Modeling Network

## Overview
A clear connection exists between Jeffrey Epstein, Ghislaine Maxwell, and the modeling industry, specifically operating through the MC2 agency and associated individuals like Paolo Zampolli, Antoine Verglas, and Jean Luc Brunel.

## Key Individuals
- **Ghislaine Maxwell:** Her early connections trace back to 1980s London where she started the **Kit Kat Club**.
- **Paolo Zampolli:** Linked to the modeling industry and potentially involved in visa matters. Mentioned in communications regarding Epstein.
- **Antoine Verglas:** Photographer and associate. Communicated with Epstein and Zampolli regarding modeling contracts.
- **Jean Luc Brunel:** Head of MC2. Deeply associated with modeling contracts and the visa pipeline.

## The MC2 Visa Factory Operations
Multiple documents indicate that MC2 was used to bring underage girls into the US under the promise of modeling contracts. Epstein and Brunel would obtain visas for these girls and then charge them rent (often at Epstein-controlled housing, e.g., 301 E. 66th).

### Visa & Contract Anomalies
- **EB-1 Petitions:** There is a notable absence of formal documentation regarding EB-1 petitions in the DOJ/EFTA documents.
- **MC2 Contracts:** Despite numerous references to MC2, there are no explicit contracts found linking individuals to the agency. 
- This lack of formal documentation raises significant questions about the legitimacy of the connections and the true nature of the operations.

### Reputation Damage
Internal documents show that association with Epstein eventually damaged the reputation and business of MC2 and Jean Luc Brunel, leading to lost revenue and visa delays.
