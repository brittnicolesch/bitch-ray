# R&C Ventures, Inc. — Financial Network and Principals Report

## 1. Executive Summary
**R&C Ventures, Inc.** operated as a financial conduit within the Ghislaine Maxwell / TerraMar Project network. Despite being justified to banks as an entity belonging to a "consultant," corporate filings and banking records reveal it was a closely held family shell company used to receive substantial, recurring wires from TerraMar and other network entities (like ELLMAX LLC).

*(Note: The financial institution utilized by R&C Ventures was **Rockland Trust Company**, which is frequently referenced in the files. "Rockford Trust" appears to be a slight misnomer for this exact banking channel.)*

## 2. Corporate Structure & Principals
According to Massachusetts Secretary of State records (File #001168658), **R&C Ventures, Inc.** was incorporated as a domestic profit corporation on **April 10, 2015**—exactly four months before it began receiving massive wires from TerraMar. 

The company was entirely controlled by two individuals operating out of a single private residence:
*   **Robert Walter Foos:** Listed as the Director, President, Secretary, Treasurer, and Registered Agent. 
*   **Caitlin Quinn Foos:** (Identified as his wife or daughter) Listed as Director and Vice President.
*   **Address:** Both the corporation and its principals list their sole address as a private residence at **299 Sam Turner Road, East Falmouth, MA 02536**.

## 3. The Rockland Trust Financial Channel
R&C Ventures did its banking through **Rockland Trust Company** (ABA# 011304478, Beneficiary Account #2962006041). The financial flow through this account was heavily orchestrated by Epstein and Maxwell's inner circle, particularly utilizing Harry I. Beller (Epstein's accountant) to channel the payments.

Key transactions include:
*   **August 6, 2015:** TerraMar Project wired a massive lump sum of **$123,108.16** directly to R&C Ventures' Rockland Trust account.
*   **October 1, 2015:** Ghislaine Maxwell personally requested that UBS set up **recurring bimonthly transfers of $3,532.52** from TerraMar's account (ending in *656) to R&C Ventures.
*   **December 1, 2015:** ELLMAX LLC (another Epstein/Maxwell associated entity) began making the same recurring $3,532.52 transfers to the Rockland Trust account.

## 4. The "Consultant" Cover Story
When UBS Bank flagged the external transfers and asked for the identity of the beneficiary, TerraMar staff (including Molly Ladd) responded on September 3, 2015, stating:
> *"R & C Ventures is an entity belonging to a consultant to the TerraMar Project. He will be receiving bi-weekly payments that will be ongoing ($3k)."* 

When UBS pushed for a specific name, the staff simply replied: **"Robert Foos."** Foos was later referred to in depositions as TerraMar's "Director of Development." 

However, no substantial work product, deliverables, or public-facing charitable initiatives have been linked to Foos or R&C Ventures. Instead, the company's incorporation timeline and massive cash influxes align perfectly with the network's broader asset purchases—including the timeline of the $130,000 cash purchase of Louella Rabuyo's condo (August 3, 2015), suggesting R&C Ventures acted as a "paymaster" or obfuscation layer for network logistics.
