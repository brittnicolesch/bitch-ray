# CASE FILE: MANOLITO ROYAL, WENDY MARTINEZ & THE YOUTH WITNESSES

### **INSIDER SUMMARY**
The management of Zorro Ranch between 2011 and 2012 was characterized by a transition from the elite "equestrian royalty" of Wendy Nun to a working-class management team consisting of **Manolito Royal ("Mon")** and **Wendy Martinez**. Their tenure was marked by increasing retaliation, a total asset lockdown, and a forced midnight exit in June 2012 that coincided with federal tips regarding human trafficking in the ranch's stables. 

Two critical youth witnesses—**Ean Royal** and **AJ Martinez**—lived on the property during this period and have since emerged as key voices in the investigation.

---

### **THE FAMILIES**

#### **1. The Royal Family**
*   **Manolito Royal:** A 17-year maintenance veteran who managed the physical infrastructure.
*   **Ean Royal (Son / Whistleblower):** Lived on the ranch for over a decade. He has publicly testified about "concrete slabs" used for skateboarding that he suspects are burial sites. He has identified areas of frequent excavation near the stables and the "Main House Estancia."
*   **Legal Status:** Manolito Royal **invoked his 5th Amendment right** in 2026 when asked to identify the high-profile male VIPs he observed at the ranch.

#### **2. The Martinez Family**
*   **Wendy Martinez:** Equestrian Director (Successor to Wendy Nun). She documented the "no horses in stables" policy in February 2012, which cleared the stalls exactly when the FBI received tips about human victims being kept there.
*   **AJ Martinez (Son / Witness):** 17 years old in 2012. He worked as a groundskeeper and cleaned mechanical rooms and the pool at the Main House. 
*   **Status:** Wendy and AJ were relocated internationally in December 2012 via funds provided by **Apollo Global Management (Leon Black)**.

*   **The "No Horses" Policy:** Wendy Martinez was banned from putting horses in stalls in Feb 2012. This policy effectively "cleared" the stables for the trafficking activity alleged in the June 2012 FBI tip.
